About

Hi there! I'm Kevin. Thanks for visiting my blog!

Adventure On is a place where I'll be journaling my adventures. The title comes from a double meaning of turning on my adventurous side and continuing to explore further. This is primarily a photo blog, but I might sneak in a thing or two about web development and design here and there.

I personally designed and developed this blog, so I hope you're liking what you see. If there are any issues you're running into, don't hesitate to leave me a message at hi@ktan.me. This blog was built using Jekyll and is hosted on Github Pages. Kudos to the authors of those tools for making my life a bit easier.

I hope you continue to enjoy your time on my blog! Have a wonderful day!
